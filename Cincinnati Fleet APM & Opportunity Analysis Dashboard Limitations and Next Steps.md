Cincinnati Fleet APM & Opportunity Analysis Dashboard Limitations and Next Steps

* The data provided by the City of Cincinnati is blank/incomplete for several variables. Some variables are entirely blank, most notably, the date the asset was officially in service. Ideally, I would guide the data collection process to ensure accuracy and robustness. But in this case, I just had to work with what was available.   
* The information collected is entirely digital based. Ideally, I would conduct some interviews, build logic trees, and apply other OA methods to get the full story. The map isn't the territory, so relying solely on digital information can be misleading, since many events happen so often that they become routine and are no longer flagged as failures.  
* The dashboard is just a tool and the project currently exists in a vacuum with no context or business question behind it. The next steps would be to use this as a step/complement in the RCA process and ultimately a report.  
* I couldn’t calculate the opportunity loss of asset failures because the financial value of assets isn’t publicly available.
